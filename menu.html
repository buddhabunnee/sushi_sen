Our Menu

Fresh and Delicious Japanese Cuisine

In 2014, Sushi Sen expanded and we now offer a full dining area in addition to our sushi bar. We have spacious booths, tables, and high-chairs to accomodate our little guests. If you are interested in watching sports events, we have mounted HDTVs with satellite service in both sections. We offer both domestic and Japanese beers, wine, and sake. Please have a look at our full sushi and kitchen menu below, where we also have download links to PDF scans of our menu.
